## What You'll Learn

- Available client management tools for MainWP
- Cost workflow and time tracking extensions
- Integration with Pro Reports for client reporting

---

Client add-ons help you extend the business side of managing WordPress sites. The built-in Cost Tracker handles core cost tracking, while add-ons add automation, time logging, and reporting workflows.
