The SEOPress for MainWP is a free extension that allows you to manage the SEO parameters of all connected Child Sites on which you have installed the SEOPress plugin. SEOPress Free must be installed and activated on your MainWP Dashboard site and at least one Child Site. SEOPress PRO is optional, but if you use it, it must also be installed and activated on the Dashboard site and at least one Child Site. The minimum supported versions are:

-   SEOPress Free on the MainWP Dashboard and at least one Child Site (5.9+)
-   SEOPress PRO on the MainWP Dashboard and at least one Child Site (5.9+, optional)
-   MainWP Dashboard (4.2.7+)
-   MainWP Child on at least one Child Site (4.2.6+)

## Install SEOPress Extension on MainWP Dashboard

The SEOPress extension is available on the WordPress repository. Installing it is just like installing a regular plugin.

1.  From inside the wp-admin of your MainWP Dashboard, navigate to **Plugins -> Add New**.
2.  Search for “**SEOPress for MainWP**”.
3.  Install and Activate the plugin.
4.  Get into your MainWP Dashboard, navigate to **Add-ons -> Posts/Pages -> SEOPress**, and start using it. ![SEOPress Dashboard page](/images/miscellaneous/add-ons-posts-pages-f01810.png)
