This article covers the most common icons you may encounter on the MainWP Dashboard.

## Sync and Check Now bulk actions

![Status icons showing red, yellow, and green indicators during bulk actions](/images/dashboard/sync-icons-2.png)

During bulk **Sync Data** and **Check Now** actions, a popup modal is displayed, and one of three icons is shown next to each site. You can hover your mouse cursor over these icons to reveal more information.

-   **Red exclamation mark** indicates that the process failed. For example, it could be due to the fact that the MainWP Child plugin was not reachable on the Child Site.
-   **Yellow exclamation mark** indicates that the process timed out. The tooltip shows **Process timed out. Please try again.**
-   **Green check mark** indicates that the process was completed successfully.

On update-related bulk actions, suspended sites are shown with a yellow **pause** icon (not a yellow exclamation icon).

## Available updates on the Dashboard site

![Red notification icon indicating available updates on the Dashboard site](/images/miscellaneous/dashboard-overview-beb979.png) You may occasionally see the red **Update Dashboard Site** label in the left navigation area (below the first-level menu, near the version label). This indicates that there is an available update (Plugin, Theme, WP Core, or Translation) on the WordPress installation where your MainWP Dashboard is installed. That includes updates to the MainWP Dashboard itself. Click that label when it appears and perform all available updates on your Dashboard site.

## Manage Sites table

![Site status icons showing red disconnected, yellow suspended, and green connected states](/images/miscellaneous/dashboard-overview-966e50.png) Different icons will be displayed next to child sites to indicate their status.

-   **Red icon** indicates that the site is disconnected, and you may click the icon to try to reconnect it.
-   **Yellow pause** icon indicates that the site is suspended.
-   **Green icon** indicates that the site is properly connected. Clicking the icon will initialize a Synchronization with that site.

## Monitoring Page table

![Site Health indicator on Monitoring page showing status states](/images/miscellaneous/dashboard-overview-22a31d.png) On the Monitoring page, the Site Health indicator shows **Good** (green) or **Should be improved** (orange). More information about Site Health monitoring is available [in this document](/sites/management/manage-child-sites#site-health-monitoring).
