The MainWP Dashboard Support modal gives you quick access to help resources directly inside the Dashboard. You can launch the AI Support Assistant, start guided tours, open page video tutorials, and jump to MainWP Support contact options.

## What You'll Learn

- How to open the Support modal from the Dashboard sidebar
- What each Support modal option does
- How to enable or disable Support Assistant, guided tours, and video tutorials
- How permissions in the modal map to MainWP Tools settings

## Prerequisites

- MainWP Dashboard installed and active
- Administrator access to your MainWP Dashboard

---

## How to Open the Support Modal

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Support from the sidebar">
Click **Support** in the left navigation panel (question-mark icon near the bottom).
</Step>
</Steps>

![Support button in the left MainWP navigation sidebar](/images/getting-started/support-button.png)

<Note>
The **Get Help** item in the top-right user menu opens the Help sidebar. It is separate from the Support modal opened by the **Support** button.
</Note>

---

## Support Modal Options

The modal includes the following cards:

![MainWP Support modal showing Support Assistant, Quick Tour, Video Tour, and Contact Support options](/images/getting-started/support-module.png)

| Option | What it does | Availability |
|---|---|---|
| Support Assistant | Opens the AI support chat inside the modal | Available when **Chatbase (AI-Powered Chat Support)** is enabled |
| Take a Quick Tour | Starts the guided tour for the current page | Available when **Usetiful (Interactive Guides & Tips)** is enabled |
| MainWP 101 Video Tour | Opens an embedded tutorial video for the current page | Available when **Youtube Embeds (Video Tutorials)** is enabled and a video is mapped to that page |
| Contact MainWP Support | Opens the MainWP community support page in a new tab | Always available |

The contact option opens: [https://community.mainwp.com/](https://community.mainwp.com/)

## Update Permissions in the Modal

Each modal card has a **Turn On / Turn Off** permission toggle (except the support contact card). Use the **Update Permissions** button to save changes.

<Steps>
<Step title="Open the modal">
Open **Support**.
</Step>
<Step title="Set toggles">
Enable or disable the cards you want to use.
</Step>
<Step title="Save">
Click **Update Permissions**.
</Step>
</Steps>

Permission toggles in the modal map to:

- **Usetiful (Interactive Guides & Tips)**: Guided tours, tooltips, and onboarding
- **Chatbase (AI-Powered Chat Support)**: AI Support Assistant
- **Youtube Embeds (Video Tutorials)**: Embedded page video guides

You can also manage the same permission toggles in **MainWP > Settings > MainWP Tools**.

## Troubleshooting

- **A card is disabled:** Enable its toggle in the modal and click **Update Permissions**.
- **Video tour is missing on a page:** Not all pages have a mapped **MainWP 101 Video Tour**.
- **Tours did not start after enabling:** Reload the page, then launch the tour again.
- **Looking for the old Need Help flow:** Use **Support** in the left navigation to open the Support modal.
